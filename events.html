Competitions

  • Jr Autonomous Robot Sumo Competition - Age Under 18 - Mini Sumo Class (500 grams)
  • Jr R/C Robot Combat Competition - No Spinning Weapons - Age Under 18 - Ant Weight Class (1 lbs)
  • Open Autonomous Robot Sumo Competition - Mini Sumo Class (500 grams)
  • Open R/C Robot Combat Competition - Ant Weight Class (1 lbs)
  • Open "You've Got Mail" Competition
  • All competitions will run in a Double Elimination format

Combat Rules

Norcal RoboRumble combat rules follow the RoboGames Combat Ruleset but implement the following changes for the competitions:

  • No use of fire or flame weapons
  • Magnets may be used for additional downforce on the steel floor but not for any other reason
  • In the case of both robots being stuck together and unable to separate, there will be 1 separation per match
  • Limit of 2 bots, per driver, per weight class
  • No Spinning Weapons (Jr Competition Only):
    • No spinning weapons of any kind, including vertical and horizontal and drum spinners.
    • All types of lifters and flippers are ok.
