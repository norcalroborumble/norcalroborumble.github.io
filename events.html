Competitions

  • Jr Autonomous Robot Sumo Competition - Age Under 18 - Mini Sumo Class (500 grams)
  • Jr R/C Robot Combat Competition - No Spinning Weapons - Age Under 18 - Ant Weight Class (1 lbs)
  • Open Autonomous Robot Sumo Competition - All Ages - Mini Sumo Class (500 grams)
  • Open R/C Robot Combat Competition - AllÂ Non-Flame Weapons OK - All Ages - Ant Weight Class (1 lbs)

All competitions will run in a Double Elimination format

Fees

  • Jr Competitions - FREE for anyone under 18
  • Open Competitions - $25 per robot per event - limit 2 robots per event per driver/operator

Schedule

9:00am Venue opens, safety check-in
10:00am Both Sumo and Combat competition fights begin
12:30pm Lunch Break
1:30pm Both Sumo and Combat competition fights resume
4:00pm Fights end, awards given
5:00pm Venue closes

All times are subject to change

Prizes

  • 1st and 2nd place for Open Sumo and Combat will receive a trophy and prizes
  • 1st and 2nd place for Jr Sumo and Combat will receive a trophy
